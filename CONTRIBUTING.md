# Contributing to Awesome AI Collective

Thanks for your interest in contributing!

## Guidelines

1.  **Alphabetical Order**: Please keep lists in alphabetical order.
2.  **Brief Descriptions**: Keep descriptions short and to the point.
3.  **Active Projects**: Prioritize projects that are actively maintained.
4.  **No Commercial Spam**: Tools must provide significant value. Pure marketing links will be rejected.

## How to Submit

1.  Fork the repo.
2.  Add your link to the appropriate category.
3.  Submit a Pull Request.
